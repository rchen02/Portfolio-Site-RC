CITI is a web-based platform that enables CITI’s enterprise clients to connect with their relationship managers and work together digitally. Clients can open accounts and track it’s progress online, view multiple account’s transaction information, visualize their global footprints and financial structure using the platform. This platform enables CITI to better manage client engagement and changed the daunting process of treasury account management into a delightful one.
